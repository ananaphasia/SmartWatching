Propelling Voice Activated Commands into the Artifical Intelligence era

This Amazon Alexa Skill learn from your actions, and then does them for you. Leaving you more free time every day.

Mission Statement

In order to best more forward into a truly hands-free and fully automated future, we have to start with small actions: such as turning the lights on and off. This Amazon Alexa skill integrates with your smart home devices, and learns when you do certain actions. Over time, this skill will become so good at its task that you won't even have to say, 'Alexa, turn off the lights'.

Endless Possibilities

Here is just some of what SmartWatcher can do, as well as some features that will be available soon.

Musical Management

SmartWatcher is a listener too! It can play your favorite music, right when you would yourself.

Light Controller

SmartWatcher can turn your lights on and off - without you lifting a finger.

Temperature Regulation

With an integrated Smart Thermostat, SmartWatcher can control the temperature in your home, whether you are there or not.

Personal Chauffeur?

Can SmartWatcher even drive your own car? Unfortunately, not yet, but this functionality is already in development.
